Who I am

My name is Carolina. I'm 20 years old, and I'm currently a sophomore in college, studying to obtain my Information Systems bachelor's degree.

I believe in a world that is increasingly productive, yet inspiring and enjoyable. And I want to be one of those who try everyday to make the world bit by bit closer to that.

What I do

I've been learning about frontend technologies since October/15, when I discovered that being a frontend developer was the best fit for me.

Ever since, I've been studying hard and refining my new skills to get an internship and start my career.
